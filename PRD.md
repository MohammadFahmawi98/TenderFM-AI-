# Executive Summary

TenderFlow FM AI is an AI-powered Tender Intelligence, Bid Management, Cost Estimation, Compliance, Proposal Generation, and Submission Management platform designed specifically for Facility Management companies.

The platform transforms facility management tendering from a manual document-heavy process into an AI-powered workflow that helps organizations:

- Analyze tenders
- Evaluate opportunities
- Generate compliance matrices
- Build manpower plans
- Calculate project costs
- Generate proposals
- Build submission packages
- Improve win rates

The platform is focused exclusively on Facility Management industries including:

- Total Facilities Management
- Hard FM
- Soft FM
- Landscaping
- HVAC Maintenance
- MEP Maintenance
- Cleaning Services
- Pest Control
- Security Services
- Waste Management
- Water Tank Cleaning
- Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)
