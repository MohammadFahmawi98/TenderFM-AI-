# TenderFM AI Project Brief

## Project Purpose

TenderFM AI is a new platform for managing tender and facilities-management workflows with AI-assisted review, organization, and decision support.

## Initial Product Direction

The first version should focus on helping users collect tender documents, understand requirements, compare opportunities, and prepare structured next steps.

## Target Users

- Facilities-management companies
- Tender and procurement teams
- Business-development teams
- Operations managers reviewing service opportunities

## Core Problems To Solve

- Tender documents are time-consuming to read and compare.
- Important deadlines, requirements, and risks can be missed.
- Teams need a clearer way to qualify opportunities before investing effort.
- Proposal preparation needs better structure and repeatability.

## First Version Goals

1. Upload or register tender information.
2. Extract key dates, requirements, scope, risks, and eligibility criteria.
3. Summarize each tender in a decision-ready format.
4. Help users decide whether to pursue, review further, or reject an opportunity.
5. Create a foundation for future proposal and workflow automation.

## Possible Core Features

- Tender document intake
- AI summary and requirement extraction
- Deadline and compliance checklist
- Opportunity scoring
- Risk and missing-information detection
- Team notes and review status
- Proposal preparation workspace
